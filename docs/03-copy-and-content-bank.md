## 11. Process Page Copy (full 6 steps)

### Step 1 · Kickoff call (free, 30 min)
No screen shares. No interrogation. We meet, you tell us what's bothering you, we tell you whether Soloex is the right fit. Low pressure on both sides.

### Step 2 · Discovery call
Now we get specific. You walk us through your actual system — we're looking at real screens, real data, real pain points. We ask the questions that matter. You'll leave this call with a much clearer picture of what needs to change.

### Step 3 · Scope and plan
We send a technical scope document: recommendations, timeline, pricing. No fluff. You know exactly what we're going to do, when, and for how much.

### Step 4 · Build
Two engagement models, your call:
- **Milestone-based** — fixed scope, weekly syncs, clear deliverables
- **Retainer** — hourly from a monthly bucket, flexible scope, continuous availability

Either way, we execute. Weekly meetings throughout.

### Step 5 · Go live
Your CRM works. Your team is trained. You're in control — not us. We hand over clean documentation and make sure everyone knows how to use what we built.

### Step 6 · Ongoing support
We don't aggressively close the relationship. Call when you need us. Tweak a workflow. Add a new integration. Build the next phase. We'll be here.
